PB&J Recipe

How to make a Peanut Butter and Jam Sandwich

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices of Bread of your choice (My choice is white bread)
  • Peanut butter
  • Jam of your choice

Recipe:

  1. Grab your bread slices and toast it (optional)
  2. After you have toasted your bread, lay them out on a plate
  3. After laying out your bread, spread an amount of your choice of peanut butter on one of your bread slices.
  4. Grab your second slice of bread and spread an amount of your choic of jam.
  5. put your bread slices together, with the jam and peanut butter facing each other.
And bam, you have your peanut butter and jam sandwich.
